About me

Hello, my name is Devin Cordek and I am an aspiring game designer currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Digital Media Production. My ultimate dream is to play a hand in designing and producing the next cultural sensation through interactive media- aka gaming! I have always been a lifelong gamer, and the virtual world is home to me almost as much as the real world is.

My experience with fictional universes, characters, stories, and settings through video games has been a motivating factor in almost everything I do, and I have recently decided to turn that lifelong passion into a life of it's own by seeking a fulfilling career in the game deign field.

Though I still have much to learn about programming and technical production, I have plenty of experience with story-writing, graphics design, music production, and game balancing, and a drive to learn so much more. I'm so excited for the future of video games, and I hope I have managed to convey that excitement to you. Thanks for reading!
